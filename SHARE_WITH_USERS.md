## Daily Use

### To Create a Quiz:
1. Launch the app (double-click `start_quiz_builder.bat`)
2. Click "Create New Quiz"
3. Add your questions
4. Click "Save"

### To Launch a Quiz for Students:
1. Select your quiz from the list
2. Click "Launch Quiz"
3. Share the displayed URL with students

### To View Results:
1. Select the quiz
2. Click "Results"
3. See all submissions with scores
